Library Design

We designed a library of sgRNA targeting human miRNAs. First, all of the human miRNA stemloop locations were downloaded from miRBase v21. To ensure sgRNAs which cleave at the ends of the stemloop were identified, an additional 20 bp before and after the stemloop was added. Then, all possible 20 nucleotide sequences followed by NGG were identified. This list of all possible miRNA-targeting sgRNAs were scored for on-target activity (Doench et al., 2014; Doench et al., 2016; Chari et al., 2015) and for off-target activity (Hsu et al., 2013). They were also aligned to the human genome (hg19) to ensure each sgRNA only targets miRNA sequences. All of this information was combined to make the 7,382 sgRNA LX-miR library which targets approximately 85% of all miRNAs with at least 4 sgRNAs each.
